Grass Toon low-poly 3d model ready for Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), games and other real-time apps.

This package contain 300 unique grass models ready to use in game or other real time application.

All assets unwrapped and textured. Easy to change diffuse color.

Included: 
-300 assets. 
-Fbx, obj and max formats. 
-8 diffuse textures with 4k and 0.5k sizes in png format. 
- Psd file.
